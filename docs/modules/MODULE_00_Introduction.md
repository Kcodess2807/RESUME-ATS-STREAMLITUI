### Part 3: Problem Statement (4 minutes)

**The ATS Challenge**:
- 75% of resumes never reach human recruiters
- Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) filter resumes automatically
- Companies receive 250+ applications per job posting
- Need for automated, intelligent screening

**Why This Matters**:
- Job seekers: Optimize resumes for ATS
- Recruiters: Save time on initial screening
- Career coaches: Provide data-driven feedback
- HR departments: Automate hiring pipeline

**Real-World Impact**:
- Helps candidates get past ATS filters
- Provides actionable feedback for improvement
- Uses AI for semantic understanding (not just keywords)
- Saves hours of manual resume review
